About the Event

The Treasure Hunt is an exciting and challenging event that tests participants’ problem-solving, teamwork, and observation skills. Teams follow clues and solve puzzles to find hidden treasures within a designated area.

Rules & Regulations

  • Participants must form teams of 2–5 members.
  • Clues will be provided at different checkpoints; teams must solve them to proceed.
  • Teams must stay within the designated hunt area; leaving the area may lead to disqualification.
  • All team members must reach the finish line together.
  • Teams must report 10–15 minutes before the start of the event.
  • Maintain sportsmanship, discipline, and cooperation at all times.
  • Decisions of the organizing committee are final and binding.
