About the Event

The Pithu Tournament is a fun and energetic outdoor game that tests agility, coordination, and teamwork. Teams compete to knock down the stacked stones and defend their base to win the championship trophy!

Rules & Regulations

  • Each team consists of 6–8 players.
  • The game begins with stacking seven stones and taking turns to hit them with a ball.
  • Teams must try to rebuild the stack without being hit by the opposing team.
  • The team that successfully knocks down the stones or eliminates opponents wins the round.
  • Teams must report 10–15 minutes before the scheduled match.
  • Maintain sportsmanship, discipline, and fair play at all times.
  • Decisions of the umpire or organizing committee are final and binding.
