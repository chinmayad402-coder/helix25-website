About the Event

The Makeup Competition is a creative event where participants showcase their artistry, skill, and innovation in makeup design. Contestants are judged on technique, creativity, and overall presentation.

Rules & Regulations

  • Each participant must bring their own makeup kit and tools.
  • The competition will have a time limit (usually 60–90 minutes).
  • Creativity, neatness, and originality will be judged.
  • Strict adherence to hygiene and safety guidelines is mandatory.
  • Participants must respect other contestants and judges at all times.
