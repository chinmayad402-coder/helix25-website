About the Event

BGMI, Free Fire, and COD Mobile are competitive mobile eSports games that test strategy, reflexes, and teamwork. Players and teams compete to eliminate opponents, complete objectives, and secure victory in fast-paced virtual arenas.

Rules & Regulations

  • Participants can compete individually or in teams depending on the game format.
  • Matches will follow standard game rules and formats as per official guidelines.
  • Participants must use their own accounts and devices; cheating or unfair practices are strictly prohibited.
  • Players must report 10–15 minutes before their scheduled match.
  • Maintain sportsmanship, respect opponents, and follow referee/organizer instructions at all times.
  • Decisions of the referees and organizing committee are final and binding.
  • Disqualification may occur for any violation of game rules, account misuse, or disruptive behavior.
