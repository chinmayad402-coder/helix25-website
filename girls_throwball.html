Throwball Tournament

Girls Event | Helix'25

About the Event

Throwball is an exciting team sport that emphasizes agility, coordination, and quick reflexes. Teams compete to score points by passing the ball over the net while preventing the opposing team from catching it.

Rules & Regulations

  • Each team consists of 7 players on the court.
  • Matches will follow standard Throwball rules and regulations.
  • Games will be played in best of 3 sets.
  • Players must throw the ball over the net; stepping over or crossing the net is not allowed.
  • Teams must report 10–15 minutes before their scheduled match.
  • Maintain sportsmanship, discipline, and fair play at all times.
  • Decisions of the referees or organizing committee are final and binding.
  • Only Team Leader details should be filled in the registration form.
