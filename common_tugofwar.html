About the Event

Tug of War is a classic team sport that tests strength, coordination, and teamwork. Two teams compete to pull the rope past a designated marker, demonstrating power, strategy, and unity.

Rules & Regulations

  • Each team consists of 8–10 players.
  • Teams will compete on a marked field with a center line and boundary markers.
  • The team that pulls the opposing team past the center line wins the round.
  • Matches may be best of 3 rounds depending on the schedule.
  • Teams must report 10–15 minutes before their match.
  • Maintain sportsmanship, discipline, and fair play at all times.
  • Decisions of the referees or organizing committee are final.
  • Only Team Leader details should be filled in the registration form.
