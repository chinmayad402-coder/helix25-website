Boys Event | Helix'25

About the Event

Volleyball is an exciting team sport that tests coordination, strategy, and agility. Teams compete to score points by spiking the ball over the net while defending their court.

Rules & Regulations

  • Each team must have 6 players on the court.
  • Matches will follow standard volleyball rules.
  • Games will be played in best of 3 sets.
  • Respect referees and opponents at all times.
