About the Event

Red Light / Green Light is a fun and exciting game that tests reflexes, speed, and focus. Participants must move quickly during the “Green Light” and stop immediately at the “Red Light” to avoid being eliminated.

Rules & Regulations

  • Participants must start behind the starting line and try to reach the finish line.
  • Movement is allowed only during the “Green Light”; players caught moving on “Red Light” will be eliminated.
  • The game will be conducted in rounds, with eliminations after each round.
  • Participants must follow instructions of the game master or referee.
  • Teams or individuals must report 10–15 minutes before their turn.
  • Maintain fair play, discipline, and sportsmanship at all times.
  • Decisions of the organizing committee are final and binding.
