About the Event

Futsal is a fast-paced indoor football game emphasizing skill, agility, and teamwork. Compete and show your soccer talent!

Rules & Regulations

  • Each team consists of 5 players.
  • Matches will be 2 halves of 10 minutes each.
  • Standard futsal rules will be applied.
  • Fair play and respect for referees are mandatory.
  • Only Team Leader details should be filled in the registration form.
