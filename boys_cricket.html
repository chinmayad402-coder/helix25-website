Boys Event | Helix'25

About the Event

The Cricket Tournament is one of the most thrilling events of Helix'25. Bring your best game, teamwork, and passion for cricket as teams compete for the championship trophy!

Rules & Regulations

  • Each team consists of 11 players.
  • Matches will follow T10 format (10 overs each).
  • Umpire’s decision will be final.
  • Teams must report 15 minutes before match time.
  • Maintain sportsmanship and discipline at all times.
  • Only Team Leader details should be filled in the registration form.
