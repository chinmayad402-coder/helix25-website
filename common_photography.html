About the Event

The Photography Competition invites participants to showcase their creativity, perspective, and technical skills through the lens. Contestants will be judged on composition, originality, and visual storytelling.

Rules & Regulations

  • Participants can submit a maximum of 3 photographs each.
  • All photographs must be original and clicked by the participant.
  • Editing and post-processing are allowed, but plagiarism is strictly prohibited.
  • Submissions must be made in digital format (JPEG/PNG) before the deadline.
  • Judging criteria include creativity, composition, lighting, and storytelling.
  • Participants must respect privacy and consent when photographing people.
  • Decisions of the judges and organizing committee are final.
